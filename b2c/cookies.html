Cookie policy

Last updated: May 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, keep you signed in, and understand how Nabad is used so we can make it better.

What we use

  • Essential cookies: keep you signed in and remember your session. Without these the app won't work.
  • Analytics cookies: measure traffic and feature usage so we can improve the product. We use anonymous, aggregated data.
  • Preference cookies: remember your settings (language, location).

We don't use cookies for cross-site advertising.

Managing cookies

You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may prevent parts of Nabad from working.

Changes

If we materially change this policy, we'll update the "last updated" date and notify you via the app or email.
