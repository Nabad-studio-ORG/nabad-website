Privacy policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Who we are

Nabad ("we", "us") is a fitness membership platform based in Beirut, Lebanon. We're responsible for the personal data described in this policy.

2. Data we collect

We collect data you give us and data generated as you use Nabad:

  • Account data: name, email, phone, password.
  • Payment data: handled by our payment processor. We never store your full card number.
  • Booking data: classes you book, attend, or cancel.
  • Device data: device type, OS, app version, approximate location.
  • Communications: messages you send to support.

3. How we use your data

  • To run your membership and process bookings.
  • To process payments and prevent fraud.
  • To send you class reminders, receipts, and product updates.
  • To improve the app and recommend studios you'll like.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Sharing

We share the minimum needed to make Nabad work:

  • Partner studios: your name and booking details when you reserve a class.
  • Service providers: payments, hosting, analytics, and email, under contract to protect your data.
  • Legal: when required by law.

We do not sell your personal data.

5. Cookies & tracking

The website and app use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember preferences, and measure usage. You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser.

6. Data retention

We keep your account data while your membership is active and for a reasonable period after cancellation to comply with legal and accounting requirements.

7. Your rights

You can request access to your data, correction of inaccurate data, deletion of your account, or a copy of your data. Email studionabad@gmail.com and we'll respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data: encrypted connections, hashed passwords, restricted access. No system is 100% secure, but we take this seriously.

9. Changes

If we materially change this policy, we'll notify you via the app or email before it takes effect.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or requests: studionabad@gmail.com.
